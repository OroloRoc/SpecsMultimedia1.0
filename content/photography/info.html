about me.

I love what I do and I truly feel I have the best job in the world...

THEOPHILUS ADJEI
 

Hey! Believe it or not, I don’t like talking about myself… well, ok a little bit…

Like most creative people, there are a few unusual things about me. You don’t really need to know about my obsession with artwork, or my past in decoration, for instance… But it might interest you to know that I am camera shy. I really don’t like getting my picture taken. Which is odd, yes, but it also means I know how to put you, your family and your guests completely at ease. But I’d much rather listen to you. And find out what you want from your special day. Which is why I always encourage clients to come and meet me and have an initial engagement session, so we can get to know each other. We can discuss the shots you really want, and how I can help you get the most out of the venue, like using the light and the setting for the best possible pictures. And finally, please be reassured that you’ll be dealing with me and me alone. This is my business, my passion. That means there’s no one else involved who doesn’t fully understand your vision for your occasion.
